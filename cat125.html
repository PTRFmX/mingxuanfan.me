Why learing Cyber Security?

Understanding the importance of cybersecurity

The present era of Internet has significantly raised people awareness of the importance of cybersecurity. For personal users, cybersecurity guarantees the safety of personal privacy information.

For companies or even governmental users, cybersecurity plays a more essential role as it effectively protects highly sensitive and classified information concerning corporational interest or national security.
